👋 About Me

Hey there! I'm Beng Brandon Che - a passionate front-end web developer and the founder of BranCodeX. I specialize in crafting clean, interactive, and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and WordPress.

My mission? To turn ideas into beautiful, functional digital experiences that make people smile and businesses grow. Whether it’s a portfolio, a blog, or a full-blown business website - I build with heart and precision.
